26 May 2020 Portrait Photography

Making Portraits

Irving Penn once wrote, "The severe portrait that is not the greatest joy in the world to the subject may be enormously interesting to the reader". This makes a photographic portrait a picture of someone who knows he is being photographed, and what he does with this knowledge is as much a part of the photograph as what he's wearing or how he looks.

What is surprising is, how you talk to your portrait subjects is more important than your gear or technique. The connection between photographer and subject creates authentic moments that no amount of expensive equipment can replicate. Enjoy a few portraits captured at different moments...

15 June 2020 Wildlife Photography

African Wildlife Adventures

African wildlife offers some of the most breathtaking photographic opportunities in the world. From the majestic lions of the savanna to the gentle giants of the forests, each moment in the wild tells a unique story of survival, beauty, and the delicate balance of nature.

Capturing these moments requires patience, respect for the animals, and a deep understanding of their behavior. The golden hours of sunrise and sunset provide magical lighting that brings these magnificent creatures to life in a way that words cannot describe.
